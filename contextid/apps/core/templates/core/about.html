The Philosophy of ContextID

In the physical world, you are a professional at work, a friend at a bar, and a patient at a clinic. Your identity is adaptive. ContextID brings this fluidity to the digital world, allowing you to resolve the right version of yourself, in the right place, at the right time.

Multi-Faceted

One account, infinite profiles. Segregate your data by context to maintain true privacy.

Resolution Engine

A powerful API-first approach to resolving identity attributes based on specific request contexts.

Modern Security

Built on the latest Django 6.0 and JWT standards to ensure your data resolution is secure.
